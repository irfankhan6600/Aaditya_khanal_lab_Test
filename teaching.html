Chemical Engineering Materials

Chemical Engineering Materials: A course exploring the properties, selection, and applications of materials in chemical engineering processes.

Prerequisite: CHEM 310 and CHEM 320.

Semesters Taught: Fall 2024.

Course Information: Syllabus

# Lecture
1 Introduction to Materials Science in Chemical Engineering.
2 Properties and Classification of Engineering Materials.
3 Thermodynamics and Phase Behavior of Materials.
4 Mechanical Properties and Material Failure.
5 Corrosion and Degradation of Materials.
6 Polymeric, Ceramic, and Composite Materials in Engineering Applications.
7 Material Selection and Design for Chemical Processes.

Thermodynamics I

A foundational course covering the principles of thermodynamics, including energy transformations, properties of substances, and the laws governing energy systems.

Prerequisite: CHEM 310 and CHEM 320.

Semesters Taught: Fall 2024.
