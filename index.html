Welcome to the Aaditya Khanal Laboratory

Geofluids lab

University of Tulsa Petroleum Engineering Building

‹ ›

At Aaditya Khanal Lab, we are at the forefront of pioneering research in Carbon Capture, Utilization, and Storage (CCUS) and Underground Hydrogen Storage (UHS), tackling critical challenges in the transition to sustainable energy systems. Our work integrates digital rock physics to enhance the understanding of fluid flow in porous media, leveraging advanced simulations, machine learning, and microfluidics experiments to uncover novel insights at both pore and reservoir scales.
